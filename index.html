DisneyPlus.com/begin



Disney Plus has established itself as a leader with its vast library of family-friendly entertainment, blockbuster movies, and exclusive series. To enjoy Disney Plus on your devices, you'll often need to activate it using an 8-digit code via Disneyplus.com/begin.


Get Your 8-Digit Code

  • Open the Disney+ app on your smart TV, streaming device, or gaming console.
  • Select the “Log In” option. An 8-digit code will appear on your screen.

Activate Your Account

  1. Open the Disney+ app.
  2. Select the “Log In” option. An 8-digit activation code will appear on your screen.
  3. On a computer or mobile browser, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  4. Type the 8-digit code shown on your TV screen into the field on the Disneyplus.com/begin page.
  5. Enter your Disney+ email and password to sign in. If you don't have an account, you can create one during this step.
  6. Follow any additional prompts to finalize the activation. Your device will now be linked to your Disney+ account.

Sign In and Start Streaming

  1. Open the Disney+ app on your device.
  2. Select the "Log In" option. If you don’t have an account, choose "Sign Up" to create one.
  3. If you're signing in, enter your Disney+ email and password.
  4. Enter the 8-digit code to link your device to your account.
  5. Browse through the extensive library of content and start watching your favorite shows and movies.
